# Logo Upload Guide for SolNet Computer Services

## Overview

The system has been updated to use your specific SolNet Computer Services logo instead of generic icons. The system now supports a **hybrid logo system** for optimal branding across different contexts:

1. **Primary Logo** - Your complete logo for website, login, and sidebar
2. **Icon Logo** - A simplified icon version for receipts, reports, and small spaces

## Steps to Upload Your Logos

### 1. Access the Business Settings

1. Log into the system
2. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Business Information**
3. You'll see two separate logo sections: "Primary Logo" and "Icon Logo"

### 2. Upload Primary Logo

1. In the **"Primary Logo"** section, click **"Choose Primary Logo"**
2. Select your complete logo image file (with company name and design)
3. Click **"Upload"** to save it
4. This logo appears on your public landing page, login screen, and sidebar

### 3. Upload Icon Logo (Optional)

1. In the **"Icon Logo"** section, click **"Choose Icon Logo"**
2. Select a simplified icon version of your logo
3. Click **"Upload"** to save it
4. This logo appears on receipts, reports, and small spaces
5. **Note**: If no icon logo is uploaded, the system automatically uses the primary logo

## Owner Photo Setup

### To Display Owner Photo on Public Landing Page:

1. Go to **Owner Profile** settings
2. Upload your owner photo
3. Enable **"Show Owner Photo"** in public information settings
4. The photo will appear in the team section of your public landing page

### Owner Photo Requirements:

- **File Type**: PNG, JPG, JPEG
- **Size**: 200x200 pixels or larger
- **Style**: Professional headshot recommended
- **File Size**: Under 5MB

## Troubleshooting

### If Logos Don't Upload:

1. Check file format (PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF only)
2. Ensure file size is under 5MB
3. Try a different browser
4. Check internet connection

### If Logos Don't Display:

1. Refresh the page after upload
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check if logos were uploaded successfully in Business Settings

### If Owner Photo Doesn't Show:

1. Ensure "Show Owner Photo" is enabled in Owner Profile settings
2. Check that the photo was uploaded successfully
3. Verify the public landing page is loading the business profile data

## Support
